About Us

At SSA SEA CARGO SERVICES LLC, Dubai, we are your trusted partner for comprehensive logistics and shipping solutions. Specializing in sea freight services to key African destinations like DR. Congo, Uganda, Tanzania, Abidjan, and Rwanda, we offer both groupage and FCL container loading, backed by our state-of-the-art warehouse facility.

Our commitment extends beyond the seas, as we provide seamless airfreight and land transportation services with a global reach. We navigate complex customs requirements, ensuring the smooth movement of your cargo, and excel in handling vehicle loading, documentation, and dangerous goods with the utmost care and compliance.

With SSA SEA CARGO SERVICES LLC, you can expect excellence in every aspect of our operations. Your cargo's safety and efficiency are our top priorities, and we look forward to serving you with professionalism and dedication.
